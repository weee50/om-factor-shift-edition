OM Factor Shift Edition
INFORMATION

If you don't know what Ordinal Markup is, you should probably go to https://patcailmemer.github.io/Ordinal-Markup/ before you play this.
This is a mod of Ordinal Markup that completely removes Factor Boosts from the game.

Main Differences from Ordinal Markup

  1. There are no Factor Boosts.
  2. Factor Shift costs have been changed.
  3. Factor Shifts no longer reduce the base.
  4. Several Booster Upgrades have been swapped and/or modified.
  5. Challenges, Incrementy, and Collapse have all been removed. (although they may be re-added later!)
